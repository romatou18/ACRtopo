ARC Team Instructions

            

                

                    
1. Installation of the app on your mobile

                    
Tap "Share" (iOS) or "Settings" (Android) and select "Add to Home Screen". This allows full offline use in the field.

                

                

                    
2. Coordinate Input/conversion

                    
Paste text from Radio logs or InReach. It handles NZTM (E/N), DDD, DMS, and DDM automatically. Converts the input coordinate to the remaining 4 formats
                        for sharing with the team, Police etc.
                    

                

                

                    
3. The Vector Feature

                    
On mobile, the app tries to get a 5-second GPS lock. If successful, it calculates the Bearing and Distance from your current location to the target.
                    Including the offset Magnetic North declination, so that you can use the bearing directly from the app/map to the compass in case, and use this app at anytime, provided you
                    have a GPS fix, and get the right Compass bearing without having to worry about manually offsetting the e.g. 23.5 deg E declination in Canterbury.

                

                

                    
4. Offline Limitations

                    
Coordinates conversion & NZTM math work 100% offline. However, Altitude and External Maps require active 3G/4G mobile data/satellite link.
                    Important: Ensure to load up the app at least once on your mobile, it gets saved for later and ensure offline functions will work on your next mission day!
